Libre Software Meeting in Bordeaux - July 6th through 10th

The fifth LSM will start in a couple of weeks. TLDP members Emma Jane Hogbin, Machtelt Garrels, Ismael Olea, Sergiusz Pawlowicz and Guylhem Aznar will be representing and promoting TLDP in the South of France. They will talk about the workings of TLDP and take advantage of the occasion to try and engage new volunteers. iBiblio will also send a delegation, and the French and German LDP will probably be represented as well.

Find out more at http://www.libresoftwaremeeting.org.
