A serious hardware failure crashed www.geekbone.org and mirrors.geekbone.org on April 24.
The major data, including Debian mirror got lost and it probably will take about one month to recover.
We are very sorry about this and will try our best to bring everything back online as soon as possible.

4月24日，www.geekbone.org 及 mirrors.geekbone.org 经历了一次严重的硬件故障.
主要数据，包括Debian镜像在内都遭到了破坏. 恢复这些数据将花费大约1个月左右的时间。
于此我们非常抱歉，我们将努力在尽短的时间内将整个系统恢复正常。
